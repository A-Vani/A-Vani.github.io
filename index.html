Akash Vani

Reserach Intern

Max Planck Institute for Astrophysics

Heidelberg University

Hi, I am Akash! I am a research intern at the Max Planck Institute for Astrophysics, Garching, Germany. Currently, I am trying to understand the redshift evolution (0<z<6) of various galaxy scaling relations derived from different observations in a semi-analytical galaxy evolution model. I completed my master's degree from Heidelberg University in September 2022 and worked on population synthesis of white dwarfs in a semi-analytical Milky Way model with Prof. Andreas Just at the Astronomisches Rechen-Institut (ARI), ZAH, Heidelberg.

I am on the lookout for a PhD position and I am interested in researching the evolution and dynamics of galaxies including the study of the Milky Way.

Interests

  • Formation, dynamics and evolution of galaxies
  • Dynamics and evolution of the Milky Way using nearby stars
  • Semi-analytical modelling
