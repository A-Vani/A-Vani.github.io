The Fifth Catalogue of Nearby Stars

Last updated on Mar 26, 2023
CNS5 CMD

The aim of this work was to construct and explore a catalogue of all stars withins 25pc around the Sun. I primarily did an early analysis of the completeness limit and the luminosity function and later explored the local stellar density variations and the properties of the stellar content of the catalogue. This work was later published in A&A.
